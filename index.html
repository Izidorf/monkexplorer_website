About the Book

This comprehensive guide covers everything from the glacial lakes of the Gorenjska region to the emerald rivers and narrow Alpine gorges of Primorska, the intermittent and underground lakes of Notranjska, the warmer rivers amid the primeval forests of Dolenjska, the magical remote glacial valleys of Koroška, and the hidden waterfalls of the Pohorje plateau in Štajerska. Most of these destinations are within a two-hour drive from Ljubljana.

If you’re visiting Slovenia or just passing through, don’t miss out on discovering one of these breathtaking locations.
